Conclusion

As I mentioned earlier, I'm not a data scientist. I would like to be one. However, I've always enjoyed looking at data. I truly believe that by visuallizing data you can bring it alive and tell a story. This is probably one of my larger data projects and I felt that I added much more to it than I have in the past. I used Bokeh, Matplotlib, D3.JS, webscraping and Pandas to do some analysis.

However, since about 2014 hate crimes, against Muslims, is rising. Could this have something to do with ISIS? Trump ran for President in June of 2015. Did the help fuel the fire?
